LoreTrip

The project management system and a network that helps create an interactive learning process for school children

Hello! The idea behind LoreTrip is to get school children involved in projects which would get them to go and explore their surroundings, especially outdoors while using modern technology. LoreTrip encompasses a set of apps for teachers and their students. Teachers can easily create different tasks for children to solve and later monitor and manage the process of the project. Tasks can involve taking pictures, recording sounds and videos, which children could easily upload and later edit them on the website through easily controllable and colourful apps. Later projects can be shared with friends and parents. LoreTrip is also a network for exchanging ideas for new or existing successfull projects, helping teachers and their students from different schools communicate with each other and spread ideas about new ways of learning and discovering the world we live in.

FAQ

What does LoreTrip stand for?
LoreTrip stand for knowledge gained through exploration.
Who is LoreTrip designed for?
LoreTrip is design as a tool for teachers, to help them create engaging, interactive projects. It has very simple interface and can be used by children from 7 years old with teachers guidence.
