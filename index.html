When people get older the exercise becomes non-existent or a rare activity only practiced by a few, despite well-known facts that regular physical activity has many health and lifestyle benefits. People with active lifestyle feel healthier and are less likely to suffer from heart disease, stroke, cancer or diabetes. If you want to get healthier and stay fit activfits helps you find a perfect training program, a perfect fit to enhance your daily life and overall health, whether it is outside walks, swimming, jogging, Tai Chi or exercising whilst sitting on the chair.

WHAT IS ACTIVFITS?

We all know that exercising helps us to enjoy energetic independent life, but activfits helps you track your sports activities, improve with professional help, make new acquaintances and be a part of internet community. It is a source for fitness and health that is created to suit the needs of the elderly. You just have to set your preferences and a personal training program will be generated, with lots of recommendations, exercising videos and access to training, nutrition and many other professionals. Everyone is also encouraged to look for exercise buddies, organize sports and health events and share stories.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Just create an account, download our health tracking apps or sync other tracking devices with our website. To get personalized training suggestions you have to take a test and answer a few simple questions like: "How often would you like to exercise?". All users get access to articles about health and fitness in older age, suggestions about exercise rutines and nutrition. You will be able to upload daily information from tracking devises and set personal goals. Data will be analysed and saved to help track the progress of your active life: performance, weight, heart rate, etc. You will also get recommendations on how to improve. Premium users will get access to hundreds of videos and exercise programs specialy created for elders.
