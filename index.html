Elders are not a tribe that has to stay at home and wait for the end. One day we all going to be a part of that tribe and it does not mean that when we reach a certain age we have to abandon active, healthy and social lifestyle. If you have health issues or just want to stay fit, activFITS helps you find a perfect training program a perfect fit, to enhance you daily life and overall health, whether it is outside walks or exercising whilst sitting on the chair. We all know that exercising helps us to enjoy energetic independent life, but activeFITS helps you improve with professional help and make new acquaintances and be a part of community.

WHAT IS ACTIVFITS? activFITS is a source for fitness and health that is created to suit the needs of the elderly. You just have to set your preferences and a personal training program will be generated, with lots of recommendations, exercising videos with access to professional help. It also encourages people to look for exercise buddies, organize and share sports events and share their stories. activFITS offers an easy and inexpensive way to track your health, set goals , get professional help.
